Log Temps

Log the temp.
Earn the points.

Drop a temp reading in seconds. GPS detects your spot, slide to set the temperature, tag the conditions and any hazards. Done.

  • GPS auto-detects your swim spot
  • Tag conditions: calm, choppy, rough, extreme
  • Report hazards: jellyfish, seals, sharks

  • Link your WhatsApp group for coordination
