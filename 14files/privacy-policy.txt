# PRIVACY POLICY

**Last Updated:** January 27, 2026

SwimLoading ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information in compliance with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA) of South Africa.

## 1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We collect the following personal information:

**Account Information:**
- Email address
- Password (encrypted)
- Display name
- Full name
- Phone number
- Date of birth
- Home address

**Emergency Contact Information:**
- Next of kin name and contact details
- Relationship
- Medical conditions and allergies

**Swimming Data:**
- Water temperature logs
- Location data (GPS coordinates and beach names)
- Swimming times and distances
- Swim event participation
- Swimming ability level
- Cold tolerance preferences

**Usage Data:**
- Device information
- IP address
- Browser type
- Usage patterns and preferences

## 2. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

We use your information for:

**Safety Purposes:**
- Emergency contact notification
- Location tracking during swims
- Medical information for emergency services

**Service Delivery:**
- Account creation and management
- Swim event organization
- Community features
- Performance tracking

**Communication:**
- Service updates
- Safety alerts
- Swim event notifications
- Community messages

## 3. LEGAL BASIS FOR PROCESSING

Under POPIA, we process your information based on:
- Your explicit consent
- Legitimate interests (safety and service delivery)
- Contractual necessity
- Legal obligations

## 4. DATA SHARING

We do NOT sell your personal information.

We may share your information with:

**Emergency Services:**
- Police, ambulance, NSRI in case of emergency
- Your designated emergency contact

**Service Providers:**
- Supabase (database hosting - EU servers)
- Email service provider

**Other Users:**
- Display name and swimming activity (public)
- Location and temperature logs (public)
- Profile photo (if uploaded)

Your emergency contact details and medical information remain PRIVATE.

## 5. DATA STORAGE

Your data is stored on Supabase servers located in the European Union. We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption and access controls.

## 6. DATA RETENTION

We retain your data:
- Active accounts: For the lifetime of your account
- Deleted accounts: 12 months after deletion (for legal compliance)
- Anonymized data: Indefinitely for statistical purposes

Our service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you are under 18, you must have parental consent to use SwimLoading.

We use essential cookies for:
- Authentication
- Session management
- User preferences

You can disable cookies in your browser, but this may affect functionality.

## 10. DATA SECURITY

We implement security measures including:
- Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS)
- Encrypted password storage
- Access controls and authentication
- Regular security audits

However, no system is 100% secure. You use SwimLoading at your own risk.
